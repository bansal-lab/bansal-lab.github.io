We are a bioinformatics research group based in the Division of Genome Information Sciences, Department of Pediatrics at the University of California, San Diego. We have strong ties with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and the Bioinformatics and Systems Biology program.

Our broad goal is to use high-throughput sequence data in conjunction with computational and statistical methods to advance our understanding of the genetic basis of human disease and human genetic variation. Research in the group involves the development of computational methods, implementation of software tools and large-scale data analysis and interpretation.
