Vikas Bansal (Principal Investigator)

Vikas is an associate professor in the Department of Pediatrics, School of Medicine at UC San Diego. He is also an affiliate faculty in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. He has received training in Computer Science (IIT Delhi), Bioinformatics (Ph.D. at UCSD) and Human Genetics (Scripps Research Institute).
