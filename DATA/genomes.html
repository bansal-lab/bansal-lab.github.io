This page documents datasets for population-scale whole-exome and whole-genome sequencing from diverse human populations. These datasets may be useful as controls in the analysis of genomes from patients with rare or even common complex diseases. 

ESP exomes 6500 4300 europeans, 2200 african americans, allele frequencies available  http://evs.gs.washington.edu/EVS/
finland exomes 1500 allele frequencies only for 1500 exomes http://sisuproject.fi/search
Danish exomes (T2D) 2000 genotype counts and allele frequencies  http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S000292971300520X
UK10K exomes/ low coverage genomes                         4000 WGS-lowcov and 2000 exomes requires data access agreement http://www.uk10k.org/data_access.html
Japanese exomes 1200 genotype counts http://www.genome.med.kyoto-u.ac.jp/SnpDB/download.html
Netherlands GoNL project 500  allele frequencies for 500 individuals  http://www.nlgenome.nl/?page_id=9
Wellderly (Scripps) 480 genotype counts for CGI whole-genomes www.stsiweb.org/SWGR/
South Asian exomes (UK) 147 exomes, low coverage whole-genomes http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sra/ERX412192%5Baccn%5D
1000 GENOMES 2500 2500 exomes from 5 continents ftp://ftp-trace.ncbi.nih.gov/1000genomes/ftp/
