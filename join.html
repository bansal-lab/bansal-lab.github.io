Open position for postdoctoral research/Bioinformatician. We have a position, open immediately for a postdoctoral research or a Bioinfomatician to work on methods for long-read sequencing technologies in the context of human genomes. This position is funded by a NIH grant. Please send me your CV and a brief statement of research interests by email.

Ph.D. students in the Bioinformatics and Systems Biology (BISB) graduate program who are interested in doing a rotation or joining the lab are strongly encouraged to contact me. We have a number of research projects available. We are looking for new Ph.D. students starting in Fall 2020 to work on a NIH funded project on methods for long-read sequencing technologies.

We are affilated with the Computer Science and Engineering department and a member of the Bioinformatics faculty within the CSE department. Ph.D. students in the CSE program are encouraged to contact me for thesis projects.

Prospective students interested in working with me are encouraged to apply to the BISB Ph.D program or the CSE Ph.D. program. Information about research projects for UCSD graduate students is available here

Undergraduate students enrolled at UCSD and interested in doing research for 10-12 hours per week are welcome to contact me. Please send an email with your CV, information about coursework relevant to Bioinformatics (CSE 181, CSE/BENG 182, etc).
